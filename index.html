About

Rodrigo Antonio Rojas Mardones (Chile, 1976) is a multidisciplinary artist based in Ratingen-Homberg, Germany. With a background in Design and formal studies in graphic printing systems and acrylic painting, he has integrated creative practice with a parallel career in Quality Assurance since 2015.

Since 2018, he has been deeply involved in the study of Astrology, adding a symbolic and cosmological dimension to his work. His practice spans painting, sculpture, woodwork, and digital media.
